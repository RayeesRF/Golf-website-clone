ABOUT US

Home to a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range, powered by Toptracer Range technology. Complimented by a practice green and bunker, coffee shop and American Golf Store. There truly is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses.

TopRacer Range

TopRacer Range is an advanced golf driving range experience with interactive games, data tracking, and fun for all skill levels.

Adventure Golf

Adventure Golf offers a fun, themed miniature golf experience with creative obstacles and challenges suitable for all ages.

Golf Lessons

Golf Lessons provide personalized instruction to improve skills, covering techniques, swing, and strategy for golfers of all levels.
